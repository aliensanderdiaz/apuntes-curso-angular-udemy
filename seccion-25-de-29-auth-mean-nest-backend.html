Sección 334 de 404 - configurar mongoDb docker

Encender docker desktop

https://hub.docker.com/ 

                  
            > touch docker-compose.yml
          
                  
            version: '3'

            services:
              db:
                container_name: mean-db
                image: mongo:5.0.16
                volumes:
                  - ./mongo:/data/db
                ports:
                  - 27017:27017
                restart: always
          
                  
            > docker compose up -d

Poner en el gitignore la carpeta de mongo
